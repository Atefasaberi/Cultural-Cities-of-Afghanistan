Bamyan

Bamyan, located in central Afghanistan, is famous for the ancient Buddha statues carved into the cliffs, which once stood as the tallest in the world. The region is also known for the stunning Band-e Amir lakes and its rich Hazara culture. Tourists love Bamyan for its peaceful atmosphere, traditional food, and breathtaking mountain views.Thre famous things in Bamyanis:-

  • Famous for Buddha statues
  • Band-e Amir lakes
  • Known for natural beauty
