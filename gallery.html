Afghanistan is a land of diverse cities, each with its own stories, traditions, and cultural richness. Explore some of its most beautiful cities here.

Herat

Herat is known for its ancient citadel, poetry, and rich Islamic architecture.Three famous things inHerat is :-

  • Herat Citadel (Qala Ikhtyaruddin) - historic fortress from ancient times.
  • Islamic Architecture- Beautiful mosques and tilework from the Timurid era.
  • Poetry and Literature- A center of Persian poetry, art, and calligraphy.

Kabul

Kabul is known for its vibrant bazaars, historical sites, and diverse culture.Three famous things in Kabul is :-

  • Gardens of Babur – A beautiful historical garden from the Mughal period.
  • Bird Street & Bazaars – Traditional markets full of colors, sounds, and culture.
  • Cultural Diversity – Kabul reflects a mix of many Afghan ethnic groups and traditions.
